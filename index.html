Fruits

Apple

Apples are rich in dietary fiber and Vitamin C. Apple comes in variety of colours like red, green and golden. Their tastes also vary as some apples are sweet and some are tangy. In India, Kashmir and Himachal are the main areas which are famous for apple production.

Banana

Banana is one of the most commonly eaten fruit in the whole world. This is a favorite and nutritional fruit. (2) Banana is yellow colored fruit. But the raw banana is green in color, and after ripping, its color changes to yellow.

Orange

Oranges are round orange-coloured fruit that grow on a tree which can reach 10 metres (33 ft) high. Orange trees have dark green shiny leaves and small white flowers with five petals. The flowers smell very sweet which attracts many bees. An orange has a tough shiny orange skin that holds acid in outside layer.
